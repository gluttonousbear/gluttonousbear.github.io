Ingredients (1 Serving)

600g chicken
100ml barbecue sauce
15ml olive oil
1 medium onion 1 tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp.brown sugar OR honey

Spices

cayenne pepper

This is very easy to make. All you need is a big pot and patience. The flavors come out very distinctive in this one, as the long cooking time allows the meat to assimilate all spices.
Preparation
Cut up chicken breast into medium sized chunks. Form doesn't matter, so this can be done quick. Chop onion. Throw all ingredients into a big pot, add some water and let lightly boil on medium for 3-4 hours. After that take two forks and pull that chicken apart. Let simmer for another hour or until the water has evaporated and all that's left is saucy, steamy BBQ-chicken.
